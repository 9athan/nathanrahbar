Formula SAE Project

FSAE is one of the largest competitions of its kind in the world, bringing together students from dozens of countries across the globe. As competitors, we put in hundreds of hours of hands-on engineering as we design, build, and test our race car from the ground up, all while being full-time students at Cal Poly Pomona.

I am part of the Electrical Team, specifically the Accumulator Team. I have a strong focus on designing and devloping PCBs while being cautious of High Voltage concerns.

Accomplishments

  • Designed accumulator board in Altium
  • Built around High Voltage lines while meeting space requirements
  • Integrated temperature sensors to ensure our battery stays at a normal temperature
