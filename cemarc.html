CEMARC

Autonomous recovery avionics for a competition-grade launch vehicle.

Role: Avionics

Overview

I contributed on the Avionics team designing and integrating telemetry and recovery electronics for CEMARC’s autonomous descent system. Work spanned component trade studies, harness/PCB design, and integration.

Accomplishments

  • Built trade matrices for radios, sensors, and compute; selected parts for reliability and BOM efficiency.
  • Drafted wiring diagrams and prelim PCB layouts for power/telemetry blocks; iterated with mech/flight teams.
  • Defined battery management approach and protection; verified interfaces and grounding strategy.
